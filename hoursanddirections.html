Sunshine Country Day School

Sunshine Country Day is located in the heart of East Walpole, directly across from Bird Park and inside Saint Mary's Parish Center. This ideal location allows the children to be exposed to a diversity of community activities. For example, we are within walking distance to East Walpole Post Office, local bank and pizza parlor. Along with these exciting community field trips we have the beautiful 89 acre Bird Park. Bird Park exposes the children to the natural outdoor environment; frequent trips to the park help instill and appreciate nature.

Sunshine on the Common

Sunshine on the Common is our second school located in Walpole center near the beautiful town common. We can be found inside the United Church in Walpole, directly next door to the Walpole Center Post Office. This new location brings us close to the town library as well as both the fire and police stations.
