About us and our Program

The Sunshine Schools of Walpole are know as one school with two locations. Both Sunshine Country Day School and Sunshine on the Common are year round day care & preschool centers. We provide care for infants, toddlers, preschool and pre-kindergarten aged children.
Sunshine is owned and operated by Jennifer Quinn, a lifelong resident of Walpole. Jen and the Sunshine Team have created a unique philosophy that promotes self-esteem, individualism, and positive reinforcement. The entire Sunshine family is committed to preparing our children for a lifetime of learning.
Sunshine consists of six developmentally age appropriate classrooms within three programs: Infant / Toddler Program, Preschool Program & Pre-Kindergarten Program.
