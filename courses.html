Tumble Bus

We are so lucky that every week the Tumble Bus drives over to our school. The children get to let our some energy while learning how to take turns and move and play!

Musicals

The Senior class performances are some of the highlights of our school year! They perform a holiday musical as well as an exciting spring concert. With lots of practice and great excitement students learn and perform songs and dances for their school friends, teachers, parents and friends in the community! It's a favorite for all!

Intergenerational Activities

Sunshine has a great relationship with New Pond Village Senior Living facility. The students in our pre-K classroom visit with the elderly on a regular basis, singing songs and playing games with them. The relationship extends to include the New Pond Village residents in our concerts and other fun activities.

Yoga With Kristen

Our favorite yoga instructor - Kristen - visits Sunshine every week. Yoga helps even our littlest friends learn about patience and calm.

Tunes for Tots

Our whole school lights up when Ms Cindy comes to sing and play music with all of our friends in our program called Tunes for Tots! Cindy plays her guitar and brings excitement to music. Our students and teachers regularly listen to music and have fun dance parties so they are ready to sing along with Ms Cindy!
